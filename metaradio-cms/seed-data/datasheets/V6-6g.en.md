# Challenge · The compound challenge brought by 6G's integration

**Title:** 6G is no longer "the next generation"—it presses every capability onto one map
**Description:** The loss introduced by high frequency bands, the reconfigurable propagation introduced by RIS, ISAC making the communication link also a sensing link, NTN pushing the network into the sky, and AI-native channels demanding large-scale labeled training data. Each direction is hard on its own; stacked together they demand a unified EM modeling and twin platform even more.
